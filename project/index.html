Founder OF Pakistan

1876 - 1948

Missile Man of Pakistan

Quaid-e-Azam Muhammad Ali Jinnah was a great leader and the founder of Pakistan. He was born on December 25, 1876, in Karachi. He worked hard for the rights of Muslims in the Indian subcontinent and led the struggle for a separate nation. Through his strong leadership, dedication, and vision, Pakistan came into being on August 14, 1947. He is known as the "Father of the Nation." His famous motto was "Unity, Faith, Discipline." Quaid-e-Azam passed away on September 11, 1948, but he will always be remembered for his efforts and sacrifices for Pakistan.
